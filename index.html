About UniFlow

UniFlow is an open-source student productivity platform designed to help university students manage schedules, tasks, assignments, and related files in one centralized system.

Open-Source Statement

UniFlow is a free and open-source software project. Users and contributors are welcome to use, study, improve, and share the software.

Features

  • Scheduling and calendar support
  • To-do list management
  • Assignment tracking
  • Related files organization
  • Announcements and reminders
